Here are Top Businessmen in India (2025)

Mukesh Ambani

Mukesh Ambani is one of Indias most prominent and influential businessmen. Born on April 19, 1957, in Aden, Yemen, he is the eldest son of the legendary industrialist Dhirubhai Ambani, founder of Reliance Industries. Mukesh studied Chemical Engineering in Mumbai and briefly pursued an MBA at Stanford University before returning to India to join the family business. Today, he is the Chairman and Managing Director of Reliance Industries Limited (RIL), a Fortune Global 500 company and India's most valuable private sector enterprise.Under his leadership, Reliance transformed from a petrochemicals and refining company into a diversified conglomerate with interests in telecom, retail, and digital services. In 2016, he launched Reliance Jio, which disrupted the telecom industry by offering free calls and affordable internet, making India one of the world’s largest data consumers. He also expanded Reliance Retail, now one of Indias largest retail chains. Recently, Mukesh Ambani has focused on green energy, investing in solar, hydrogen, and battery technologies to make Reliance a net-zero carbon company by 2035.As of 2025, his net worth is estimated at over $110 billion, making him one of the top ten richest individuals globally. He resides in Antilia, a 27-story private residence in Mumbai, considered one of the most expensive homes in the world. Married to Nita Ambani, a philanthropist and educationist, Mukesh has three children—Akash, Isha, and Anant—who are actively involved in the family business. His contributions to Indias economic growth and technological progress have earned him global recognition and admiration.
