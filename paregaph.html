INFORMATION OF CHHATRAPATI SHIVAJI Maharaj

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was a great warrior and the founder of the Maratha Empire in western India. was born on 19th February 1630 at Shivneri Fort to Shahaji and Jijabai . Known for his bravery, intelligence, and leadership, he built a strong kingdom based on the idea of Swarajya (self-rule). Maharaj was coronated as Chhatrapati in 1674 at Raigad Fort . He is famous for his use of guerilla warfare tactics, strong fort management, and building Indias first modern navy to protect the coastline. He respected all religions and was known for his just and inclusive administration. His escape from Agra and victory against Afzal Khan at Pratapgad are legendary. Shivaji Maharajs life and work continue to inspire generations as a symbol of courage, good governance, and patriotism.
